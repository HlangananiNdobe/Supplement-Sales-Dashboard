Supplements Sales Dashboard (2020-2025)
Dashboard Overview
This dashboard analyzes sales performance across multiple product categories, locations, and years. It provides insights into revenue trends, units sold, returns, and country performance, allowing users to filter data dynamically by year and product category.
This project demonstrates my ability to work with data modeling, Power BI visuals, DAX, and dashboard design to solve real business problems.

Tools & Techniques
•	Power BI Desktop
•	Data Cleaning & Modeling
•	DAX Measures
•	Slicers for filtering
•	Custom Formatting & Theme
•	KPI Cards

Dashboard Features
•	Key Performance Indicators (KPI Cards)
o	Total Revenue
o	Units Sold 
o	Units Returned 
•	Bar Chart: Total Sales by Product Name
•	Pie Chart: Sales revenue across countries
•	Horizontal Bar Chart: Total Sales by Category
•	Line Chart: Sales Trends Over Time (202-2025)
•	Dynamic Filters (Slicers): Year (2020–2025).
Insights
•	Total revenue reached 22.91M across all years.
•	Canada delivered the highest share of sales.
•	Protein and vitamin products contributed significantly.
•	Clear revenue growth trend from 2020 to 2025.
•	Only 6714 units were returned, which indicate strong product quality.
